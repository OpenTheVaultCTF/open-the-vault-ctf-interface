What is Capture the Flag?
CTF also known as Capture the Flag is a cyber security game related to hacking and security. It can be played as either a team game or as an individual game. There are different types of CTFs. The players can use any method necessary to complete the levels in the CTF.
About Open the Vualt CTF
Open the Vualt containt both shell and the web base CTF box challenge How can one patch up a vulnerability that they do not even know exist?
